Pork Adobo

Description

This is a recipe post for Filipino Pork Adobo. It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic. There are version wherein onions are also added.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs oil
  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbs pepper corn
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 2 tbs sugar
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate cubed pork belly in soy sauce and garlic for at least 1 hour
  2. Add pork belly in hot oil in pot and saute until browned, save marinade
  3. After the pork belly has browned, add marindate to pot
  4. Fill pot until water is ~3/4 the way from covering the pork belly, then add bay leaves and pepper corns
  5. Bring to boil, and then simmer until meat is tender for ~40 minutes
  6. When meat is almost tender, add vinegar and simmer uncovered for 10 minutes. Do not mix pot during this time
  7. After 10 minutes, salt to taste and serve hot
