Beef Nilaga

Description

Beef Nilaga or Nilagang Baka is literally translated as “Boiled Beef”. This is a simple soup dish best served during rainy and cold weather.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs oil
  • 2 lbs fatty cut beef such as chuck
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tbs pepper corn
  • 4 tbs fish sauce patis
    • 2 ears of corn
  • 2 large potatoes
  • 1 bokchoy or 1/2 head of cabbage
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Add cubed beef in hot oil and saute until browned
  2. Add water until there is ~2 inches of water above beef
  3. Bring to boil, lower to simmer, and skim off impurities
  4. Add chopped onion, pepper corn, and fish sauce to pot, bring to boil, then simmer
  5. When beef is tender, add corn and simmer for 10 minutes
  6. When corn is 5 minutes away from being done, add potatoes and simmer
  7. When potatoes are almost fork tender, add bokchoy or cabbage, simmer for 2 minutes
  8. Salt to taste and serve hot
